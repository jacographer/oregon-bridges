Highway River Bridges in Oregon, centered on Eugene

Made by Jacob Saindon

This map shows all of the bridges in Oregon which carry highways over waterways, centered on the city of Eugene in west-central Oregon. Located along the Willamette River, Eugene features a number of highway river bridges. Additionally, the McKenzie River splits from the Willamette just north of Eugene. This page includes photos of the Spores bridge, which crosses the McKenzie to the north of Eugene, and the Jasper Bridge, which crosses the Willamette to the southeast of Eugene. This map is a valuable resource for those looking to find accessible historic bridges, river views, and (potentially) fishing spots.

Project assets

Data is shown on Mapbox web map with geolocation services enabled. Bridge data downloaded from Oregon Spatial Data Library. Bridges shown were selected from a 100ft buffer from stream vector data (USGS National Geospatial Program Small-Scale Data), and limited to those carrying a highway ('HWY').
